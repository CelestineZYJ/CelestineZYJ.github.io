I am a fourth-year PhD student at Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). Before that, I received my BEng degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, in 2020.

Research

I have broad interests in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computational Social Science. Especially, I focus on the following directions:

  • Dynamic natural language understanding (e.g., temporal adaptation). I am interested in exploring temporal adaptation methods for language models facing evovling environments over time.
  • I am recently focusing on how to effectively and efficiently acquire real-world knowledge to equip large language models.
